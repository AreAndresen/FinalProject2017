The Problem

From a technological perspective, everything that is too time consuming is a problem. Paying invoices is something that takes unnecessary lot of time. This is a problem that should be solved more efficient with the right technological solution.

Have you ever experienced receiving new invoices in the mail or through email and had to take your time to sit down and pay them? Then you have most likely typed in the long Customer Identification Number, account number, amount and so on. Payment of new bills and invoices are often time consuming and problematic. Our opinion is that this can be simplified and solved in a more elegant and less troublesome way. Now this is where QR-faktura enters the stage!

Our research

We have put together a survey to find out what people would think of a solution like QR-faktura. The survey tok place in november 2017. It was around 100 participants, and the total survay had nine questions. We picked out the most relevant. We use the survey’s results to emphasize that there is a need of such a solution. Here are the results:

What method do the participants use to pay bills?

The majority of the participants use their computer and a smaller group uses their mobile phone. May the reason for this be the lack of technological tools for the mobile phone considering use of time and usability?

Do u use "eFaktura" or "AvtaleGiro" to receive and pay bills?

We asked our participants if they were using eFaktura and/or AvtaleGiro to receive and pay invoices. With a share of 78 percent, the majority uses the services. eFaktura and AvtaleGiro has to already be established for regularly payments and does not relate to first-time and irregularly invoices. An option to sign an agreement with eFaktura and AvtaleGiro will be a possibility in our solution.

What do you think of the amount of time you spend paying your bills?

The majority of the participants think that they spend too much time paying their bills. 65% want a easier and faster solution! This question gives us overview of the necessity for an improvement of the technological solution. This can be solved with accessible and usable apps. We want to turn the trend, so people can spend less time on paying bills!

Would you have used a service that fills out all information in an invioce?

This question reveals the need of a service that automatically fills in the information, makes it easier and saves you the time! With this survey we got the suspicion confirmed. There is a great need for improvement within invoice technology.
