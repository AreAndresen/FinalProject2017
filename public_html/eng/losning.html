The Solution

The solution is an application that makes it possible to pay bills just by scanning a QR-code. This means that you can avoid using unnecessary time to plot in long digits such as KID numbers and account numbers.

We want to make it possible to pay bills just as easy as scanning a QR-code. When you register an account with QR-Faktura you have to fill in your username(email), full name, and create a personal password. This specific part of the login is only necessary once, until you manually log out of the application. 

In the top left corner of the app there is an icon “settings”. Here you can choose which bank you are affiliated with. The app will then communicate with the chosen bank, and your bank accounts at this bank. This gives the user the opportunity to choose which account number they wish to complete the transaction with. QR-Faktura can be compared to the already existing solution, Vipps. When QR-Faktura is used to scan the QR printed on the paper invoice, the information and context is automatically filled into the app.



Solution continues after the illustration

When the scan is complete the user has the opportunity to check if the information is correctly filled in. If everything is in order, just plot in the pin-code given by your bank, and the personal password that you made when you registered your account with QR-Faktura. These two passwords are implemented to safeguard the accounts of our users, and to limit the consequences incase someone gets their mobile phone stolen.
After the entry of the pin-code and personal password the payment will be complete, and the user will get a confirmation “Faktura er betalt”. Then the app will automatically ask the user if they want to create an “Avtalegiro” with the sender of the bill. Simultaneously this gives the user the possibility to fill in necessary premises for this agreement, as max-amount, and date for future transactions with this corporation. All registered agreements will at any given time be available at “Avtalegiro” within QR-Faktura. This makes it easy so the user can go in and terminate any given agreement, as they please.The team in QR-Faktura agree that this is a far more efficient, and better solution than what is available at the market today.
It is that easy to pay with QR!

Privacy


User safety is one of the main concerns for QR-Faktura. We want the users to experience that paying invoices with the QR technology feels both safe and easy. Since sensitive information is required, privacy and security is very important. Due to this fact, we have focused on a secure login, through a bankID and a personal password, as final steps to complete your transactions. This makes the solution solid in terms of security and excludes unintended users to misuse your QR-Faktura account. QR-Faktura is safe and easy to use.


QR-faktura make use of cookies. A cookie is an information capsule that lies on a web server. Cookies are not used to run programs or deliver viruses to your mobile phone or computer. The main purpose of a cookie is to save and preserve informasjon that you have already filled in. This gives the user the opportunity to save time by not repeating the same information twice, for example when you log in and out.

As a user you have the ability to deny the use of cookies, but will at the same time lose the benefits that these provide. Cookies increase effectiveness and user satisfaction when using our application. As an additional security measure we recommend our users to log out of QR-Faktura, when sharing their mobile phone.

Responsive design


Responsiveness is the ability of the website to adapt to different devices and sizes so the website still makes sense. The key to responsive design is to not use any absolute sizes in our code. Instead, we use relative sizes that adapt to different components. But even with relative sizes, a website will not be optimalized on devices such as mobile phones. Elements can appear a little too small and therefor website will not be universally designed. Because of that, we have designed our website a little differently when using devices such as mobile phones.

We have designed the mobile version in accordance to screens and windows with a max-width of 768 pixels. Elements would be too small and indistinctly in a horizontal navigation bar on a mobile phone. Hence the reason why we have designed a vertical navigation bar for devices such as a mobile phone. The same goes with the footer.

Usability: accessible for everyone


Our website is designed for the best fit regardless of who is using the website. The key is to design the website in a way that all people are able to navigate and use the website. The website must be designed for people who are visually or motorically impaired, color blind, technological difficulties and other challenging disabilities. In other words, the website should be organized and easy to navigate and it should never take more than three “clicks” to find what you are looking for. We have tested our website at the European Internet Inclusion Initiative which tests accessibility and HTML validation.


We have used the guidelines for universal design from the WCAG 2.0 document to make sure that the website is universally designed.
